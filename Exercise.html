Exercise

Q: Dogs and cats ___ natural enemies.

Solution

Dogs and cats are natural enemies.

Q: Dad ___ a newspaper.

Solution

Dad is reading a newspaper.

Q: Dave ___ the car yesterday.

Solution

Dave drove the car yesterday.

Q: Jeff ___ Paris with his friend before.

Solution

Jeff has visited Paris with his friend before.

Q: I ___ as a pizza delivery guy during summer break.

Solution

I was working as a pizza delivery guy during summer break.
